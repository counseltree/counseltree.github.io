About Counseltree

We're a team of four Northwestern Law students focused on making legal education accessible to small businesses so they can address legal issues and get back to running their business.


Narcés Beltran

Narcés worked as senior lawyer at Scotiabank Group in Bogotá. In this position, he worked as a legal advisor in commercial litigation and represented the bank before jurisdictional and regulatory authorities. He has also also worked in law firms and the public sector.
Fun Fact: Enjoys jazz, blues and music festivals.


James Larsen

James worked for four years in marketing and sales management with Gravity Payments and Vulcan, Inc. He decided to pursue a legal education to facilitate the communication between attorneys and business leaders.
Fun Fact: Wrote a scene for the TV show “Portlandia” that starred Paul Allen and members of the Portland Trail Blazers which aired in 2014.

Tony Traina

Tony previously worked as a healthcare consultant at Navigant Consulting. He decided to attend law school after realizing no strategy is complete without a holistic understanding of the legal risks associated with an industry.

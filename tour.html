Take a Tour

Counseltree operates in just a few simple steps. Get to know us here.


Step 1: Submit a Question

Give us some basic information about your business and the legal question you have. The more information you give us, the more tailored information we can provide.

Step 2: Relax

Sit back, relax, and let Counseltree get to work. We'll gather the research and resources you need to become educated on the legal issues you've asked us about.

Step 3: Receive Your Answers

Within 24 hours, you'll get an email from a Counseltree staff member. It will provide a summary of the research conducted to help educate you make the most informed decision possible. It will also provide contact information in case you have specific follow-up questions.

Step 4: Follow Up or Share

Share the information from Counseltree with your team, directing them to follow the specific action items provided by Counseltree. Or, reach out to Counseltree with any follow-up items.
